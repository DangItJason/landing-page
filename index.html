What is Emi?

Emi is your personal AI caregiver copilot designed specifically for the unique challenges of caregiving. Built by caregivers who understand the daily struggles, Emi transforms the overwhelming chaos of care coordination into a manageable, organized system.

Key Features:

  • Care Coordination: Track appointments, medications, and care plans in one place
  • Smart Reminders: Never miss important medications or appointments again
  • Health Monitoring: Log symptoms, vitals, and progress with intelligent insights
  • Communication Hub: Coordinate with healthcare providers, family, and care team
  • Emergency Preparedness: Quick access to critical information when it matters most

Emi understands that caregiving isn't just about medical tasks—it's about maintaining dignity, fostering connection, and finding moments of peace in the storm. Our AI learns your unique situation and adapts to provide personalized support that actually helps.

About Us

EmiCare was born from lived experience. Our team consists of current and former caregivers who have walked this difficult path and understand the daily reality of caring for loved ones.

Our Mission

To empower caregivers with intelligent tools that reduce stress, improve care outcomes, and preserve the human connection that makes caregiving meaningful.

Why We Built Emi

Traditional healthcare technology wasn't designed for the complex, emotional reality of family caregiving. We've experienced firsthand the frustration of juggling multiple apps, paper records, and endless phone calls while trying to provide the best care possible.

Emi is different because it's built by people who get it. We understand that 3 AM medication questions can't wait, that coordinating care across multiple providers is exhausting, and that sometimes you just need someone to tell you that you're doing a good job.
